MicroCraft Licenses IGI Software & Products

July 26, 2019   ||    Filed under Software Announcement.

After more than 30 years of supplying custom and standard products for special applications, IGI has decided to handoff sales, development and support to companies directly involved in these applications. IGI has licensed all Electrical Test software, Inkjet Printing software and related software to MicroCraft. 

MicroCraft will also take over all existing maintenance contracts for all products. The telephone numbers for support remain the same and Mike Fulkerson is running the application and support department for MicroCraft. Gary Pontinen is running the Software Programming department for MicroCraft.

IGI continues to look for partners for other applications. If you have an interest in GenStencil technology, 3D micro/nano structures or other software technology, please contact IGI at 612.728.1300.
