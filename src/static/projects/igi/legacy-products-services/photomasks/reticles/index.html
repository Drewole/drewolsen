IGI manufactured standard and custom reticles, as well as other high-precision optical patterns, for semiconductors, MEMS, MOEMS, medical, scientific, military, and industrial use. These were made from production ready data, or by engineering specialists who designed reticles from customer concepts.

Our most commonly produced reticles were UT1X, Reduction, and 1X Stepper & Scanner reticles.
