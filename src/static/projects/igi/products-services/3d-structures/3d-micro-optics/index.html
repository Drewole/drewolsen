View Capabilities

IGI can produce a wide range of micro-optic products, including micro-lenses, micro-mirrors, gratings and light diffusers. The optics can be refractive or diffractive. We can produce aspherical and spherical structures.

IGI can build any type of formula-based structure or shape. We can produce any shape described by a table, formula, or cross-section such as:

  • Micro/Nano lens array
  • Micro/Nano lens array stamp
  • Micro/Nano lens array master
  • Custom diffusers
  • Aspheric lens array
  • Micro mirrors

Micro Lens Arrays — Regular and Random Patterns

Both spherical and aspherical lenses of a specified shape and array configuration can be created up to 100% fill.

Diffusers

We specialize in random diffusers.

Mirrors

From security features and telecommunication, to retail packaging and home entertainment, reflective micro-optics are very well known in commercial products. IGI can help you with your next micromirror or reflective micro-optic project.

Gratings

Our different 3D production technologies make gratings of almost any kind possible.

3D Applications

  • Surface Textures — anti-reflective, self cleaning, self sticking, and other biologically inspired textures — both functional and decorative
  • Micro-Optics — micro-lenses, micro-mirrors, gratings and light diffusers
  • Life Sciences — Micro/Nano textures, Bio mimicking structures
  • Micro-Fluidics
  • Micro-Text
  • Micro-Imprinting

3D Technologies

3D Shims — sculpted surfaces on any substrate in thick resist and then create a nickel master

3D Molds — sculpted surfaces on any substrate using soft lithography and laser ablation and then create a rigid mold

NanoSculpt technologies can be used together to create 3D micro-structures and sculpted patterns. Let us show you more.

  • Laser lithography and laser interference lithography
  • Electron-beam lithography for nano-structures
  • DUV Stepper
  • Measurement technology — laser confocal microscope and Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM).

3D Engineering

Don’t let a lack of engineering know-how stop your project. Use our expertise. We can:

  • Listen to your concept, design a plan for manufacture, produce your micro/nano 3D product.
  • Adapt the production process to your project’s requirements.
  • Adapt the provided data to the process.
  • Create unique microscopic 3D patterns and random contours.
  • Create prototypes by direct imaging for microstructures, to reduce R&D cycle time.
