From producibility analysis, to solder paste stencil generation, to custom process automation, to manufacture-ready outputs, IGI’s software is designed to save you resources, and time — reducing hours to minutes.

Looking to expand your capabilities in design or manufacturing, but don’t have the software to start the process? Used in house, our off-the-shelf software reflects more than 40 years of engineering and photolithography experience with varied data types across diverse industries.

Are you encountering difficulties moving from your design system to precision imaging output? Rely on our field tested software tools for a complete design-to-manufacture solution. Or, allow IGI to create custom software, blending the strengths of your current design with our precision imaging experience.

Customized Applications

Using our off the shelf software, IGI developers are expert at customizing applications to match your specific needs. We have a legacy of creating applications to support our own engineers in precision imaging, phototooling manufacture, and 3D imaging as well as developing custom solutions for others in the PCB, Electrical Test, AOI, EMS, and Solder Paste Stencil industries.

Whether you are creating prototypes, assembling/manufacturing, or designing for manufacture, our developers are experts in creating solutions for your precision imaging needs, using our field-tested, proprietary toolbox of software. We can help you actualize your ideas.
