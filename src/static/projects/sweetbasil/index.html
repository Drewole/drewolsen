Welcome to Sweet Basil Chinese Restaurant

Not Your Typical Chinese/Asian Restaurant

Sweet Basil invite you and your family to experience tasty Asian cuisine from thailand, china, and vietnam. At our restaurant, we served traditional dishes like Saigon Pho soup, spicy Papaya salad, three different types of curry dishes, and many more.. Most of our food are made mild and can be spiced up to the five levels of spicyness. Our traditional ingredients will sure wake up your taste buds and liven up your dining experience, so come on in to our restaurant and bring the family along for some delicious food! Our experienced cooks and trained staff will not dissapoint you.
