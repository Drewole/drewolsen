Sweet Basil Cooking Classes

Sundays at Noon

Pre-Registration is Required

The cooking class at Sweet Basil is a really fun and hands on experience. This class is designed with three dishes. In class you will get a chance to touch, smell and taste all the spices, herbs, and vegetables that will be used in your cooking. At the end of class you will have a chance to modify your recipes to your liking. Of course when your done cooking you get to eat the delicious food you prepared so come with an empty stomach!

Course Details

  1. Two Hour Lesson
  2. Hands on Experience
  3. Three Popular Dishes and eat them when your done
  4. Only $60 per Person
